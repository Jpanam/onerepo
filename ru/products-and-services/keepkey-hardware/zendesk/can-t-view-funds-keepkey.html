Can't View Funds - KeepKey

If you can't view funds that should be in your KeepKey, it is possible that the exchange failed and the deposited coin was auto-returned. Be sure to check your balances in both the sending and receiving wallets.

If you cannot view the funds in your KeepKey and you need some help, please contact us.
