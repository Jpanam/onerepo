Getting Started With Portis

What is Portis? 

Portis is the first compatible software wallet with the New ShapeShift Platform! A software wallet is a more convenient way of storing your crypto assets. While hardware wallets, such as KeepKey, are more secure, wallets like Portis have no cost barrier of entry, do not require a physical device, and can be logged into from any machine (as long as you remember your password)!
